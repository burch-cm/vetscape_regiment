VETSCAPE
Regiment

Vetscape is nonprofit which helps military veterans find their next mission in life. We provide training, community, and access to care for US, UK, and allied troops and their families.

This is our online gaming community: Vetscape Regiment (I'm not married to that name). The Regiment is a virtual clan of like-minded military and veteran gamers who want the fun of gaming without the hassle of adolecent swearmongers in their party. We all have different experiences in the service, but we have more in common than most; we've all been through some iteration of hell and come back.

We aim for our online gaming sessions to be a virtual team room. The brothers and sisters of Vetscape and the Vetscape Regiment have your six. Now join up, and let's kick some virtual ass.
